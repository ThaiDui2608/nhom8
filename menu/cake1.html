CAKE

The term "cake" has a long history. The word itself is of Viking origin, from the Old Norse word "kaka". It can be said that the ancient Egyptians are the ancestors of the bakery industry because there is a lot of scientific evidence to prove that their baking skills are very early. The cake was first kneaded with flour, then moistened by natural methods, compressed and baked on hot stones – at this time the pastry could be roughly called unfermented bread.
