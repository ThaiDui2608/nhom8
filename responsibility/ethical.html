Ethical Sourcing

Our success as a company has always been linked to the success of the thousands of farmers and suppliers who work diligently to grow and produce our products.

And that's exactly what we aim for as all coffees are grown to the highest quality standards, using responsible coffee growing principles, and ethical coffee trade. We believe that a better cup of coffee will also lead to a better future for farmers and a more stable climate for the earth.

Sourcing Coffee

With the help of Conservation International, we have developed ethical sourcing principles that help us source coffee responsibly grown and ethically traded.

Helping Farmers

We work directly with farmers to develop responsible farming methods, while investing in their communities to ensure a sustainable supply of quality coffee.

Sourcing Tea

We support projects that improve the livelihoods of the producers of our special Tazo® tea.

Sourcing Cocoa

Our cocoa procurement approach is designed to ensure a long-term supply of high quality and ethically sourced cocoa.
