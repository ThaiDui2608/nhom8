Environmental Management

We share our commitment to the environment with our customers

And we believe in the importance of nurturing and protecting our planet, believing in cooperation and encouraging others to do the same. As a company specializing in agricultural products, it is good business knowledge. And as people living in this world, it's simply the right thing to do.

Recycling

This is a big challenge, and we've made significant progress as well. We are working on developing eco-friendly cups and working to expand our recycling program to reduce the amount of waste we create.

Energy

We take pride in the work we've done to be more energy efficient, and we're committed to further reducing our energy consumption and using renewable energy sources to lower our impact on the planet. our spirit.

Water

We all must take care when using one of our most precious natural resources, and we will evaluate our design, equipment and storage practices to find ways to reduce water use.

Green Construct

We are transforming our stores into proper green stores using responsible building materials and energy efficient design to reduce our environmental footprint.

Climate Change

Climate change poses a serious threat to coffee growing regions around the world, and we are proactively pursuing strategies to address this and help farmers mitigate its impact.
